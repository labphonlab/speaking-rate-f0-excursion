# Highlights

- F0 excursion, not F0 maximum, contracts reliably with faster speaking rate
- F0 maximum shows no perceptually meaningful rate effect across three corpora
- The excursion effect is within-speaker in origin, driven by rising F0 minima
- The effect generalizes across English and Japanese and speaking styles
- The effect survives frame-count, loudness, and vowel-length robustness checks
